Essential Nutrients & Their Benefits

Vitamins

Essential for energy production, immune function, blood clotting and other functions.

Minerals

Important for bone health, fluid balance, and several other processes.

Proteins

Necessary for tissue repair, immune function, making hormones and enzymes.

Healthy Fats

Provide energy, support cell growth, protect organs and keep body warm.

Carbohydrates

Body's main source of energy, important for brain function and physical activity.
